All Of The Drip-Campaigns & Milestones Below Can Be Customized In The CRM

App Intake

Trigger: Status Update From ARIVE = App Intake
Loan Officer Heads Up: SMS & Email with steps to opt out of milestone updates.
Borrower: Gentle 30-day check-in sequence to remind borrowers you’re here to help.

App Completed

Trigger: Status Update From ARIVE = App Completed
Loan Officer Heads Up: SMS & Email with steps to opt out of milestone updates.
Borrower: Receives an email and text requesting documents with direct upload links to Arrive.

Opt Out Pre-Screen

Trigger: Status Update From ARIVE = App Completed
Loan Officer Heads Up: SMS & Email with steps to opt out of milestone updates.
Borrower: The Opt-Out Prescreen Campaign is triggered after a borrower completes their loan application, designed to prompt them via email or text to opt out of solicitations from other lenders.

Pre-Approved (Up to 6 Months)

Trigger: Status Update From ARIVE = Pre-Approved
Loan Officer Heads Up: SMS & Email with steps to opt out of pre-approval follow up.
Borrower: The Pre-Approved Campaign helps loan officers stay top-of-mind, build borrower trust, and drive deals forward with automated follow-ups, agent coordination, and timely engagement.
Buyer’s Agent (90 Days): Notifies buyer’s agents at pre-approval and follows with 90 days of check-ins—keeping them informed and reinforcing the your role as a reliable, proactive partner during the home shopping phase.

Not Yet Ready

Trigger: Status Update From ARIVE = Archived Loans
Loan Officer Heads Up: SMS & Email with steps to opt out of not yet ready follow up.
Borrower: Nurtures not yet ready contacts with long-term, value-driven content—keeping the LO top-of-mind and positioning them as a trusted advisor until the borrower is ready to move forward.
