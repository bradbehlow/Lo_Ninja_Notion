Borrower: Quick SMS & email reminder that they need to e-sign disclosures before moving forward.
Underwriting - Borrowers
Trigger: Status Update From ARIVE = Underwriting

Borrower: Automated update that lets borrowers know when the file has officially been submitted to underwriting, and when it’s under review.

Trigger: Status Update From ARIVE = Underwriting

Agents: Automated update that lets realtors know when the file has officially been submitted to underwriting, and when it’s under review.
Approved - Borrowers
Trigger: Status Update From ARIVE = Approved
Loan Officer Heads Up: SMS & Email with steps to opt out of milestone updates.
Borrower: Messages go out once conditional approval is received. Borrowers are reminded to upload remaining documents.
Approved - Buyer’s & Seller’s Agent
Trigger: Status Update From ARIVE = Approved
Loan Officer Heads Up: SMS & Email with steps to opt out of milestone updates.
Agents: Messages go out once conditional approval is received. Agents are updated so everyone knows the loan is moving forward—just pending final items.
CTC - Borrowers
Trigger: Status Update From ARIVE = Clear To Close
Loan Officer Heads Up: SMS & Email with steps to opt out of milestone updates.
Borrower: This milestone celebrates reaching CTC! Borrowers and co-borrowers receive a congratulatory message and a quick note on what happens next. Helps build excitement and clarity before the closing table.
CTC - Buyer’s & Seller’s Agent
Trigger: Status Update From ARIVE = Clear To Close
Loan Officer Heads Up: SMS & Email with steps to opt out of milestone updates.
Agents: The agent gets notified that the file is ready to wrap up and a quick note on what to look forward to—giving them confidence and time to coordinate closing.
Docs Out - Borrowers
Trigger: Status Update From ARIVE= Docs Out
Loan Officer Heads Up: Email with steps to opt out of milestone updates.
Borrowers: They are notified when their final loan documents have been sent out, providing clarity and avoiding confusion about the loan's status.
Friday Updates - Buyer’s Agents
Trigger: Friday evening for loans in underwriting or approval status.
Loan Officer Heads Up: Email with steps to opt out of milestone updates.
Agents: Every Friday, agents receive a polished, professional status update covering where each of their deals stands. It’s fully automated but feels personal—and helps you build trust and repeat business without extra effort.
